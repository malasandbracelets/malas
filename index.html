Our Mission

At Heart in Hand, we hope to inspire you, by providing you with tools to empower and evoke change. Stories and malas are our passion, as they are both tools that help to lead you in, connect you with yourself, and help guide you through growth.

Do you remember the last time you felt wonder? We hope to help you touch that place in yourself that is abundant, unreserved and willing to believe in the impossible. Through a combination of stories that spark a connection and malas for self-exploration, we hope to inspire you to journey in. We are a small home based mala company in Victoria, BC, and we believe in the power of healing through story and that stories are medicine. All pieces are handmade with intention, care and some level of customization in every piece. Pick between completely custom bracelets and necklaces or from a series of story “collection” pieces. Every mala or piece of jewellery is unique, stories are ethically sourced and in some cases pieces are recycled.

What We Offer

Simply put, malas are a strand of 108 beads created with created with intention, traditionally used for meditation. Like most tools that begin to lead you inward, there are many ways that they can be used. Made from quality materials such as seeds, stones and wood, malas are consistent touch point and beautiful reminder of your own voice, whether for setting goals, protection, relaxation, or simply just to wear, our jewellery is one of a kind.

We offer some level of customization across all pieces. Pieces include either mala style necklaces or bracelets created from seeds, stones or wood beads. There are two options to choose from when ordering from us, series mala or custom malas. Custom pieces take colour preferences and intentions into account when building your piece. Series pieces are inspired from specific stories with some level of colour preference, they are dynamic in their intentions by combining a piece of custom jewellery with a story and its meaning. Thank you for choosing Heart in Hand.
