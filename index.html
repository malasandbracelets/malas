Our Mission

Heart in Hand Malas creates tools to inspire and empower you in evoking change. Stories and malas lead you inward, connecting you with yourself. May you guide your own growth.

Do you remember the last time you felt wonder? We hope to help you touch that place in yourself that is abundant, unreserved and willing to believe in the impossible. Through a combination of stories that spark a connection and malas for self-exploration, we hope to inspire you to journey in. We are a small home-based mala company in Victoria BC, and we believe in the power of healing through story and that stories are medicine. All pieces are handmade with intention, care and some level of customization. Pick between completely custom bracelets and necklaces or from a series of story “collection” pieces. Every mala or piece of jewellery is unique, stones are ethically sourced and in some cases pieces are recycled.

What We Offer

Simply put, malas are a strand of 108+1 beads that are created with intention and traditionally used for meditation. Originating in the East, these beads inspired what would later become prayer beads. The word ‘bead’ in English at one point carried the meaning ‘prayer’, and humans have been using forms of this adornment for intention across cultures for centuries. Like most tools that begin to lead you inward, there are many ways that they can be worked with. Made from natural high quality materials, malas are a consistent touch point and beautiful reminder of your own voice. Whether for setting goals, protection, relaxation or simply just to wear, our jewellery is one of a kind.

Heart in hand puts love into every mala we make. In order to do that we need to get to know you better. By finding out about your needs we can work with you to create a tool that is functional and meaningful. Different levels of customization are possible, and depending on the product selected pieces can be made from seeds, stones, or wood beads. While similar, both options have slight differences in engagement and creation.

Custom malas focus on color preferences and intentions. Series malas are inspired by specific stories, and allow for at least one central colour selected by you. Series malas are also uniquely dynamic in their intentions, because they combine stone with many lines of story meaning. It can be hard to choose, but we’re here to support you on your way.
